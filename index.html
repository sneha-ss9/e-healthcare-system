About TruHealthCare Hospital

Patient centricity and best-in-class services drive TruHealthCare Hospital. Established in 1987 by Ch. Aishi Ram Batra Public Charitable Trust, the hospital has an infrastructure of 500 beds, 14 operation theatres, 110 ICU beds, 24x7 emergency facilities, state-of-the-art diagnostic labs, and rehabilitation facilities. TruHealthCare Hospital, the first multi-speciality private hospital in Delhi, aims to bring world-class medical care within reach of everyone.

Over the years, TruHealthCare Hospital has advanced in areas like Cancer, Cardiac, Orthopaedics, Nephrology, and Neuro Surgery, offering the latest medical care in critical and life-threatening fields.

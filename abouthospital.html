About TruHealthCare Hospital

TruHealthCare Hospital, established in 1987 by Ch. XYZ Public Charitable Trust, is dedicated to patient-centric and quality healthcare. With 500 beds, 14 operation theatres, 110 ICU beds, and 24x7 emergency facilities, we aim to provide top-notch medical services accessible to all.

Our specialties include Cancer, Cardiac, Orthopaedics, Nephrology, and Neurosurgery, backed by the latest technology and highly skilled staff.
